description: |
  TuneHunter helps DJs and music buyers find where to purchase the tracks they already want—faster, across multiple stores in a single search.
  Digital music purchasing is fragmented. DJs often need to manually search across multiple platforms to compare availability, pricing, and versions of the same track.
  Unlike store-specific search or manual multi-tab workflows, TuneHunter aggregates multiple music stores into one interface optimized for fast, buyer-focused discovery.
